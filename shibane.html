Auto Liquidity

Auto Liquidity refers to a portion of each transaction being automatically added to the liquidity pool. This mechanism helps maintain price stability and ensures there is enough liquidity for smooth trading.

50%

Native Reflection

Native Reflection means a percentage of each transaction is automatically redistributed to all token holders. This incentivizes holding the token, as it increases the balance of existing holders without any additional action.
